About Majestic Events

A decade of crafting luxury event environments across the UK.

"With over 10 years of experience, Majestic Events specialises in creating refined, luxury event environments. From elegant décor to bespoke lighting, every detail is carefully designed to deliver a seamless and unforgettable experience."

Founded in 2015, we began with a simple belief: that every event — no matter its scale — deserves to feel extraordinary. Over the years, that conviction has shaped everything we do, from the way we listen to our clients in our very first consultation, to the care we take in placing the final finishing touch on the day itself.

We operate across Manchester, the Midlands and London, working in some of the country's most beautiful venues and private homes. Our team brings together expertise in lighting design, floral styling, structural décor, and event logistics — giving you a single, trusted partner for your entire event aesthetic.

Whether you're dreaming of a grand ballroom transformation with cascading fairy lights and statement centrepieces, or an intimate outdoor ceremony lit by warm pathway lanterns, we approach every brief with the same dedication to precision, creativity, and true luxury finish.

What We Offer

Our Services

Wedding Decor

Complete venue styling designed as a unified concept — from the moment guests arrive to the final dance. Every element is carefully coordinated to create a cohesive, breathtaking environment.

  • Mehendi setups (traditional and contemporary)
  • Table styling and centrepiece coordination
  • Aisle and walkway design
  • Full venue transformation and layout planning

Bespoke Wedding Lighting

Lighting designed to shape atmosphere, highlight architectural features, and enhance the overall experience. We study each venue individually and build a lighting plan unique to your day.

  • Venue-specific lighting design
  • Ambient and mood lighting
  • Feature lighting for focal areas
  • Integration with décor and layout

Outdoor Light Walkways

Illuminated pathways designed to guide movement and create a strong visual entrance. Perfect for evening receptions, outdoor ceremonies, and garden celebrations.

  • Entrance pathway lighting
  • Garden aisle illumination
  • Directional flow design
  • Evening and low-light optimisation
